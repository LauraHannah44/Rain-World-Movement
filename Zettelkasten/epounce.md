# epounce - Extended Slide-Pounce
![[epounce_header.gif]]
# Description:
A variant of a [[pounce]], produced by pressing jump during frames 27 through 31 (after the throw input) of an [[eslide]]. It is always possible to [[pounceroll]] from this move.
