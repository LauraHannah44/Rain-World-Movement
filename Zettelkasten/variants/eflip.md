# eflip - Extended Slide-Flip

# Description:
A variant of a [[flipping]], produced by pressing backwards and jump during frames 27 through 31 (after the throw input) of an [[eslide]]. It is always possible to [[roll]] from this move.

# Notes:


# Performed from:
[[eslide]]

# Allows for:
[[downthrow]]

# Chains to:
[[walking]], [[roll]]
