# turnflip - Turn-Flip
![[turnflip_header.gif]]
# Description:
By pressing jump within the first 9 frames of a [[dashturn]], slugcat will enter the [[flip]] state. Unlike flip moves from a [[slide]], it is possible to vary your jump arc using input directions during this move.
