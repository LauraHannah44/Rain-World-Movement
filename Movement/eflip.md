# Extended Slide-Flip
![[eflip_header.gif]]
## Description
A variant of a [[flip]], produced by pressing backwards and jump during frames 27 through 31 (after the throw input) of an [[eslide]]. It is always possible to [[fliproll]] from this move.

[[eslide]]
